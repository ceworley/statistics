  1. What is the probability of spinning 30? In other words, what is ℙ[X=30]\mathbb{P}[X=30]?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 22 or 26? In other words, what is ℙ[X=22 or X=26]\mathbb{P}[X=22 ~\text{ or }~ X=26]?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 22 and then spinning 26? In other words, what is ℙ[X1=22 and X2=26]\mathbb{P}[X_1=22 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=26]?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at most 7? In other words, what is ℙ[X≤7]\mathbb{P}[X\le 7]?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using μ=∑x⋅ℙ[x]\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x].
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using σ=∑(x−μ)2⋅ℙ[x]\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}.
