  1. What is the probability of spinning 18? In other words, what is ℙ[X=18]\mathbb{P}[X=18]?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 23 or 25? In other words, what is ℙ[X=23 or X=25]\mathbb{P}[X=23 ~\text{ or }~ X=25]?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 23 and then spinning 25? In other words, what is ℙ[X1=23 and X2=25]\mathbb{P}[X_1=23 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=25]?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at least 23? In other words, what is ℙ[X≥23]\mathbb{P}[X\ge 23]?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using μ=∑x⋅ℙ[x]\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x].
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using σ=∑(x−μ)2⋅ℙ[x]\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}.
