Exam 2

  1. Question

    In some situation, each trial has 0.58 probability of success. There will be 7 trials. (Thus the number of successes will follow a binomial distribution.)


    1. What is the probability of getting exactly 4 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X=4]\mathbb{P}[X = 4].
    2. What is the probability of getting exactly 2 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X=2]\mathbb{P}[X = 2].
    3. What is the probability of getting more than 2 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X>2]\mathbb{P}[X > 2].
    4. What is the probability of getting at least 2 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X≥2]\mathbb{P}[X \ge 2].
    5. What is the probability of getting less than 2 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X<2]\mathbb{P}[X < 2].
    6. What is the probability of getting at most 2 successes? In other words, determine ℙ[X≤2]\mathbb{P}[X \le 2].
    7. Determine the mean number of successes.
    8. Determine the standard deviation of successes.
