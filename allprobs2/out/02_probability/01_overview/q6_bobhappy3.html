  1. Question

    Bob has a 0.3 chance of winning a game. If Bob wins, he has a 0.8 chance of being happy after the game. If Bob loses, he has a 0.33 chance of being happy after the game.

    After the game, you notice Bob is happy. What is the probability that Bob won his game? (Do not answer as a percentage; answer as a decimal.)
