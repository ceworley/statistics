We have rephrased our problem into a standard normal probability problem, because

P(|J−170|<30)=P(140<J<200)=P(−1<Z<1)=P(|Z|<1)P\left(\big|J-170\big| < 30\right) ~=~ P(140<J<200) ~=~ P(-1<Z<1) ~=~ P\left(\big|Z\big|<1\right)
