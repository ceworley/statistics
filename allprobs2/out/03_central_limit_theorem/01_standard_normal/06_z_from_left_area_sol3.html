    Start with a sketch. Leftward numbers (toward −∞-\infty) will be less than our boundary zz, so we shade a left region with area 0.17.


    You should go to your zz-table and find the zz-score with the left area closest to 0.17.

    zz P(Z<z)P(Z<z)
    -0.98 0.1635
    -0.97 0.166
    -0.96 0.1685
    -0.95 0.1711
    -0.94 0.1736
    -0.93 0.1762

    It turns out the exact answer is z=−0.9541653z=-0.9541653, which could be found by using an inverse normal function. On a spreadsheet:

    =Norm.Inv(0.17,0,1)

    Using R:

    qnorm(0.17)

    But, the zz-table is accurate enough, so I will accept either -0.96 or -0.95 (anything within 0.01 of -0.9541653).
