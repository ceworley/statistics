    Method 1: Determine the area of each tail. Both tails have the same area, and all three areas add to 1. Thus, P(Z>z)=1−0.42=0.3P(Z>z) = \frac{1-0.4}{2} = 0.3

    P(Z<z)=0.3+0.4=0.7\begin{aligned} P(Z<z) &= 0.3+0.4 \\ &= 0.7 \end{aligned}
