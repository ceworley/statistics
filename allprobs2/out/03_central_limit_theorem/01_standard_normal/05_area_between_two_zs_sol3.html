We take a difference of areas. P(−0.91<Z<0.82)=P(Z<0.82)−P(Z<−0.91)=0.7939−0.1814=0.6125\begin{aligned} P(-0.91<Z<0.82) &= P(Z<0.82) - P(Z<-0.91) \\ &= 0.7939 - 0.1814 \\ &= 0.6125 \end{aligned}



In a spreadsheet, you could use the NORM.DIST() function.

=NORM.DIST(0.82,0,1,TRUE) - NORM.DIST(-0.91,0,1,TRUE)

In R, you could use the pnorm function.

pnorm(0.82) - pnorm(-0.91)
