1. solution_03_central_limit_theorem/01_standard_normal/05_area_between_two_zs

    Start with a sketch.


    We take a difference of areas. P(−0.85<Z<0.51)=P(Z<0.51)−P(Z<−0.85)=0.695−0.1977=0.4973\begin{aligned} P(-0.85<Z<0.51) &= P(Z<0.51) - P(Z<-0.85) \\ &= 0.695 - 0.1977 \\ &= 0.4973 \end{aligned}



    In a spreadsheet, you could use the NORM.DIST() function.

    =NORM.DIST(0.51,0,1,TRUE) - NORM.DIST(-0.85,0,1,TRUE)

    In R, you could use the pnorm function.

    pnorm(0.51) - pnorm(-0.85)
