Start with a sketch. The total two-tail area is 0.68, so each tail has half that area.


Method 1: Determine the area of each tail and the center. Both tails have the same area, and all three areas add to 1. Thus, P(Z>z)=0.682=0.34P(Z>z) = \frac{0.68}{2} = 0.34

P(Z<−z)=0.682=0.34P(Z<-z) = \frac{0.68}{2} = 0.34

P(|Z|<z)=1−P(|Z|>z)=1−0.68=0.32\begin{aligned} P(|Z|<z) &= 1-P(|Z|>z) \\ &= 1-0.68 \\ &= 0.32 \end{aligned}

Find the left area.

P(Z<z)=0.34+0.32=0.66\begin{aligned} P(Z<z) &= 0.34+0.32 \\ &= 0.66 \end{aligned}
