σ∑X=σXn=(5.7662813)144=69.1953756\begin{aligned} \sigma_{ \sum X} &= \sigma_{ X} \sqrt{n} \\ &= (5.7662813)\sqrt{144} \\ &= 69.1953756 \end{aligned}

The central limit theorem tells us that the random sum is approximately normal with the parameters calculated above.
