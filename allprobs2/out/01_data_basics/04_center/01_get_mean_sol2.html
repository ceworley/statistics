    You need to sum the values (∑𝐱\sum \mathbf{x}, see summation) and divide by the sample size (nn).

    x‾=∑𝐱n=585.712=48.8083\bar{x} = \frac{\sum \mathbf{x}}{n} = \frac{585.7}{12} = 48.8083 You can round x‾\bar{x} to the nearest tenth: 48.8.
