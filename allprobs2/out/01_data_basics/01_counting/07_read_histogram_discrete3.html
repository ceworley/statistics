    A standard eight-sided die was rolled 70 times, and the results were organized into the histogram shown below. In dice notation, we could say the results of 70d8 were plotted as a histogram. (Pedantic sidenote: it is common to interpret 70d8 as the SUM of 70 rolls, but we will interpret 70d8 as the LIST of 70 rolls and use ∑70d8\sum 70\text{d}8 as the sum of 70 rolls.)
