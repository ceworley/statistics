A standard eight-sided die was rolled 20 times, and the results were organized into the histogram shown below. In dice notation, we could say the results of 20d8 were plotted as a histogram. (Pedantic sidenote: it is common to interpret 20d8 as the SUM of 20 rolls, but we will interpret 20d8 as the LIST of 20 rolls and use ∑20d8\sum 20\text{d}8 as the sum of 20 rolls.)



  1. Evaluate prop[𝐱<6.5]\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<6.5].
  2. Evaluate prop[𝐱>5.5]\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>5.5].
  3. Evaluate prop[|𝐱−4.5|<3]\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<3].
  4. Evaluate prop[|𝐱−6|>0.5]\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-6|>0.5].
  5. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that prop[𝐱<b]=0.8\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<b] = 0.8.
  6. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that prop[𝐱>b]=0.65\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>b] = 0.65.
  7. Evaluate integer radius rr such that prop[|𝐱−4.5|<r]=0.45\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<r] = 0.45.
  8. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that prop[|𝐱−3|>r]=0.65\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|>r] = 0.65.
