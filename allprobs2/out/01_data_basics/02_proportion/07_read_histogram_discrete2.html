A standard six-sided die was rolled 500 times, and the results were organized into the histogram shown below. In dice notation, we could say the results of 500d6 were plotted as a histogram. (Pedantic sidenote: it is common to interpret 500d6 as the SUM of 500 rolls, but we will interpret 500d6 as the LIST of 500 rolls and use ∑500d6\sum 500\text{d}6 as the sum of 500 rolls.)



  1. Evaluate prop[𝐱<3.5]\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<3.5].
  2. Evaluate prop[𝐱>4.5]\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>4.5].
  3. Evaluate prop[|𝐱−3|<0.5]\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|<0.5].
  4. Evaluate prop[|𝐱−4.5|>1]\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|>1].
  5. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that prop[𝐱<b]=0.628\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<b] = 0.628.
  6. Evaluate half-integer boundary bb such that prop[𝐱>b]=0.69\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>b] = 0.69.
  7. Evaluate half-integer radius rr such that prop[|𝐱−3|<r]=0.162\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|<r] = 0.162.
  8. Evaluate integer radius rr such that prop[|𝐱−2.5|>r]=0.688\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-2.5|>r] = 0.688.
