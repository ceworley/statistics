To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. sort(𝐱)=45.43,47.08,47.67,48.08,48.93,49.28,49.59,49.80,49.93,49.95,49.97,49.99\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 45.43, 47.08, 47.67, 48.08, 48.93, 49.28, 49.59, 49.80, 49.93, 49.95, 49.97, 49.99 Determine the minimum. min(𝐱)=45.43\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 45.43 Determine the maximum. max(𝐱)=49.99\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 49.99 Take the difference. range(𝐱)=max(𝐱)−min(𝐱)=49.99−45.43=4.56\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 49.99-45.43 = 4.56
