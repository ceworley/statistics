You could also do it the long way.

x = read.csv("get_SD.csv")$x
n = length(x)
xbar = mean(x)
deviations = x-xbar
sqrdev = deviations^2
VAR = sum(sqrdev)/(n-1)
SD = sqrt(VAR)
SD
## [1] 5.87367
