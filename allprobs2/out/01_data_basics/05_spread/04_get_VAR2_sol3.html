You could also do it the long way.

x = read.csv("get_VAR.csv")$x
n = length(x)
xbar = mean(x)
deviations = x-xbar
sqrdev = deviations^2
VAR = sum(sqrdev)/(n-1)
VAR
## [1] 36.50178
