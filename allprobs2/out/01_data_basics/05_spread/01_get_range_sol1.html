    To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. sort(𝐱)=50.28,50.33,51.15,51.41,51.56,51.74,51.85,53.10,53.97,53.99\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 50.28, 50.33, 51.15, 51.41, 51.56, 51.74, 51.85, 53.10, 53.97, 53.99 Determine the minimum. min(𝐱)=50.28\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 50.28 Determine the maximum. max(𝐱)=53.99\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 53.99 Take the difference. range(𝐱)=max(𝐱)−min(𝐱)=53.99−50.28=3.71\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 53.99-50.28 = 3.71
