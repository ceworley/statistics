A standard eight-sided die was rolled 50 times, and the results were organized into the pie chart shown below. The marks outside of the circle indicate the cumulative proportion.



  1. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<6.5]\).
  2. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>2.5]\).
  3. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4.5|<1]\).
  4. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|>1.5]\).
  5. Evaluate half-integer boundary \(b\) such that \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<b] = 0.96\).
  6. Evaluate half-integer boundary \(b\) such that \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>b] = 0.56\).
  7. Evaluate half-integer radius \(r\) such that \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-2|<r] = 0.02\).
  8. Evaluate integer radius \(r\) such that \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-2.5|>r] = 0.82\).
