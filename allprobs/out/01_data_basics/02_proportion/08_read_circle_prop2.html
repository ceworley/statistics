A standard six-sided die was rolled 20 times, and the results were organized into the pie chart shown below. The marks outside of the circle indicate the cumulative proportion.



  1. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<3.5]\).
  2. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>1.5]\).
  3. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-4|<1.5]\).
  4. Evaluate \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-2.5|>1]\).
  5. Evaluate half-integer boundary \(b\) such that \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}<b] = 0.7\).
  6. Evaluate half-integer boundary \(b\) such that \(\text{prop}[\mathbf{x}>b] = 0.3\).
  7. Evaluate integer radius \(r\) such that \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3.5|<r] = 0.5\).
  8. Evaluate half-integer radius \(r\) such that \(\text{prop}[|\mathbf{x}-3|>r] = 0.8\).
