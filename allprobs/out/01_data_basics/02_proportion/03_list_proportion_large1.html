  1. How many shots did Jordan attempt? (Evaluate \(n\).)
  2. How many shots were successful? (Evaluate \(n_\text{s}\).)
  3. How many shots were failures? (Evaluate \(n_\text{f}\).)
  4. What proportion of Jordan’s shots were successful? (Evaluate \(\hat{p}\).)
