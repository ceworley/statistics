To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 37.59, 37.62, 38.27, 40.99, 41.76, 43.15, 43.40, 43.67, 43.82, 43.83, 44.00, 44.96, 44.97 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 37.59 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 44.97\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 44.97-37.59 = 7.38 \]

x = data$x
range = max(x)-min(x)
