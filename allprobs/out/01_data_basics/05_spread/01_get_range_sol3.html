To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 40.08, 40.13, 40.20, 40.24, 40.56, 40.93, 42.94, 43.75, 44.17, 46.86, 47.51 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 40.08 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 47.51\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 47.51-40.08 = 7.43 \]
