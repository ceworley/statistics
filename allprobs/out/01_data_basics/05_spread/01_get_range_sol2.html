To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 20.17, 20.30, 21.74, 21.84, 24.43, 25.10, 28.96, 34.15, 46.19, 49.30 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 20.17 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 49.3\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 49.3-20.17 = 29.13 \]
