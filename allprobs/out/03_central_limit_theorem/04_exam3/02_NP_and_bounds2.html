Let random variable \(X\) be normally distributed with mean \(\mu=35\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=6\).


  1. Determine \(P(X>41.6)\)
  2. Determine \(P(X<37.4)\)
  3. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X>x)=0.0668\)
  4. Determine \(P(33.2<X<36.8)\)
  5. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X<x)=0.5793\)
