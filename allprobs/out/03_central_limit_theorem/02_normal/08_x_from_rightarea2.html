Random variable \(G\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 600\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 180\). \[G \sim \text{N}(600,\,180) \] Evaluate \(g\) such that \(P(G>g) = 0.22\). In other words, determine an lower boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 600 and standard deviation 180 lands over that boundary 22% of the time.
