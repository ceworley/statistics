Random variable \(K\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 79\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 18\). \[K = \text{N}(79,\,18) \] Evaluate \(k\) such that \(P(K>k) = 0.81\). In other words, determine an lower boundary such that a normal spinner with mean 79 and standard deviation 18 lands over that boundary 81% of the time.
