Method 1: Determine the area of each tail and the center. Both tails have the same area, and all three areas add to 1. Thus, \[P(Z>z) = \frac{0.48}{2} = 0.24\]

\[P(Z<-z) = \frac{0.48}{2} = 0.24\]

\[\begin{aligned} P(|Z|<z) &= 1-P(|Z|>z) \\ &= 1-0.48 \\ &= 0.52 \end{aligned}\]
