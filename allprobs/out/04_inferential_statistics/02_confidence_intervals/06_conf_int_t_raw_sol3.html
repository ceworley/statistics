x = c(541, 529, 519, 513, 501, 516, 517, 529, 529, 508, 523, 518, 518, 529, 511, 516, 523, 506, 503, 503, 520, 516, 527, 509, 505, 508, 509, 532, 502, 521, 500, 501, 545, 505, 518, 515, 502, 510, 505, 510, 501, 540, 520, 546, 526, 516, 518)
n = length(x)
xbar = mean(x)
s = sd(x)
SE = s/sqrt(n)
gamma = 0.95   # Probability T is between -tstar and tstar
cumulative = gamma+(1-gamma)/2   # Probability T is less than tstar
tstar = qt(cumulative, n-1)
ME = tstar*s/sqrt(n)
LB = xbar-ME
UB = xbar+ME
print(data.frame(n,xbar,s,SE,tstar,ME,LB,UB,row.names=""))

  1. The sample size \(=n=47\).
  2. The sample mean \(=\bar{x}=\frac{\sum x}{n}=516.57\).
  3. The sample standard deviation \(=s=\sqrt{\frac{\sum(x-\bar{x})^2}{n-1}}=12.08\).
  4. The estimated standard error \(=\text{SE}=\frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}=\frac{12.08}{\sqrt{47}}=1.76\).
  5. You can show, when \(n=47\), then \(\text{df}=46\), and \(P(|T|<2.01)=0.95\), so the boundary \(=t^\star=2.01\).
  6. The estimated margin of error \(=\text{ME}=\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}=\frac{(2.01)(12.08)}{\sqrt{47}}=3.55\).
  7. The lower boundary of the confidence interval \(=\text{LB}=\bar{x}-\text{ME}=516.57-3.55=513.03\).
  8. The upper boundary of the confidence interval \(=\text{UB}=\bar{x}+\text{ME}=516.57+3.55=520.12\).
