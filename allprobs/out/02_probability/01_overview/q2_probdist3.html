  1. What is the probability of spinning 21? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=21]\)?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 4 or 5? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=4 ~\text{ or }~ X=5]\)?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 4 and then spinning 5? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X_1=4 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=5]\)?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at most 11? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X\le 11]\)?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using \(\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x]\).
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using \(\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}\).
