This scenario describes a permutations problem (order matters). We are considering the nonrepeating sequences of size 5 from a set of size 17.

\[\begin{aligned} {_nP_r} &= \frac{n!}{(n-r)!} \\\\ n &= 17 \\\\ r &= 5 \\\\ {_{17}P_{5}} &= \frac{17!}{(17-5)! } \\\\ &= \frac{17!}{12!} \\\\ &= 17\cdot16\cdot15\cdot14\cdot13 \\\\ &= 742560 \end{aligned}\]
