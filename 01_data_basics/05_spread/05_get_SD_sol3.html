\[\bar{x} = \frac{\sum \mathbf{x}}{n} =36.9 \]

Find the mean of the squared deviations.

\[\text{VAR}_\text{biased} = \frac{\sum (\mathbf{x}-\bar{x})^2}{n} = 4.686625 \]

Take the square root of the variance.

\[\text{SD}_\text{biased} = \sqrt{\frac{\sum(\mathbf{x}-\bar{x})^2}{n}} = \sqrt{4.686625} = 2.1648614\]

R

You can do this with R

x = read.csv("get_SD.csv")$x
xbar = mean(x)
deviations = x-xbar
sqrdev = deviations^2
VAR_biased = mean(sqrdev)
SD_biased = sqrt(VAR_biased)
