To determine the range by hand, you subtract the minimum value from the maximum value. You could first sort all the data. \[\text{sort}(\mathbf{x}) = 36.47, 38.27, 40.96, 41.11, 41.85, 41.94, 42.63, 43.15, 43.48, 43.59, 43.78, 44.55, 44.96 \] Determine the minimum. \[\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 36.47 \] Determine the maximum. \[\text{max}(\mathbf{x}) = 44.96\] Take the difference. \[\text{range}(\mathbf{x}) = \text{max}(\mathbf{x})-\text{min}(\mathbf{x}) = 44.96-36.47 = 8.49 \]
