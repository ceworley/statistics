  1. (ANS=0) The proportion of measurements between 83.5 and 84.5 is 0. \[\text{prop}[83.5<\mathbf{x}<84.5]=0\]
  2. (ANS=0.6) The proportion of measurements outside 84.5 and 88.5 is 0.6. \[\text{prop}[\texttt{NOT}(84.5<\mathbf{x}<88.5)]=0.6\]
  3. (ANS=0.3) The proportion of measurements closer than 1 cm from 85.5 cm is 0.3. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-85.5\big|<1\right] = 0.3\]
  4. (ANS=0.3) The proportion of measurements farther than 3.5 cm from 84 cm is 0.3. \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-84\big|>3.5\right] = 0.3\]
  5. (ANS=3) You can guess and check until you find an integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 3\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-86.5\big|<3\right] ~=~ 0.7\]
  6. (ANS=0.5) You can guess and check until you find a half-integer that satisfies the equation. It turns out that \(r = 0.5\) satisfies the equation, because \[\text{prop}\left[\big|\mathbf{x}-87\big|>0.5\right] ~=~ 0.9\]
