Let random variable \(X\) be normally distributed with mean \(\mu=50\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=6\).


  1. Determine \(P(X<40.4)\)
  2. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X>x)=0.7257\)
  3. Determine \(P(42.8<X<57.2)\)
  4. Determine \(P(X>61.4)\)
  5. Determine \(x\) such that \(P(X<x)=0.8413\)
