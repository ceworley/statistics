Random variable \(X\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu=30\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=8\). \[X \sim N(30, 8)\]


  1. If \(n=121\), determine \(P\left(\overline{X}<28.96\right)\)
  2. Determine \(P(X>35.4)\)
  3. If \(n=81\), determine \(P\left(\sum X <2487\right)\)
  4. If \(n=25\), determine \(P\left(\overline{X}>31.78\right)\)
  5. Determine \(P(X<39)\)
  6. If \(n=100\), determine \(P\left(\sum X >2991\right)\)
