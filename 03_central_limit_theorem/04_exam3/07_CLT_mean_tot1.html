Random variable \(X\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu=70\) and standard deviation \(\sigma=5\). \[X \sim N(70, 5)\]


  1. Determine \(P(X<70.9)\)
  2. If \(n=100\), determine \(P\left(\sum X <6924\right)\)
  3. If \(n=49\), determine \(P\left(\sum X >3398\right)\)
  4. Determine \(P(X>76.6)\)
  5. If \(n=36\), determine \(P\left(\overline{X}<71.05\right)\)
  6. If \(n=121\), determine \(P\left(\overline{X}>70.19\right)\)
