03_central_limit_theorem/02_normal/10_bounds_from_twotailarea_v2

Random variable \(M\) is normally distributed with mean \(\mu = 79\) and standard deviation \(\sigma = 18\). \[M = \text{N}(79,\,18) \]


  1. Evaluate \(d\) such that \(P\left(\big|M-79\big|>d\right) = 0.74\). In other words, determine the distance from mean of two boundaries such that this normal spinner lands outside those boundaries 74% of the time.
  2. Let \(m_1 = 79-d\), such that \(P\left(M < m_1\right) = \frac{0.74}{2}\). Evaluate \(m_1\).
  3. Let \(m_2 = 79+d\), such that \(P\left(M > m_2\right) = \frac{0.74}{2}\). Evaluate \(m_2\).
