You are tasked with determining the confidence interval (of the population mean) with the confidence level of \(\gamma=0.98\).


  1. Determine \(n\), the sample size.
  2. Determine \(\bar{x}\), the sample mean.
  3. Determine \(s\), the sample standard deviation (with Bessel correction).
  4. Calculate \(\frac{s}{\sqrt{n}}\), the estimated standard error.
  5. Determine \(t^\star\) such that \(P(|T|<t^\star)=0.98\). Remember, \(\text{df}=n-1\).
  6. Calculate \(\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}\), the estimated margin of error.
  7. Calculate \(\bar{x}-\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}\), the lower boundary of the 98% confidence interval.
  8. Calculate \(\bar{x}+\frac{t^\star s}{\sqrt{n}}\), the upper boundary of the 98% confidence interval.
