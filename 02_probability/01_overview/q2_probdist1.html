  1. What is the probability of spinning 15? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=15]\)?
  2. What is the probability of spinning 17 or 29? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X=17 ~\text{ or }~ X=29]\)?
  3. If spinning twice, what is the probability of first spinning 17 and then spinning 29? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X_1=17 ~\text{ and }~ X_2=29]\)?
  4. What is the probability of spinning at least 6? In other words, what is \(\mathbb{P}[X\ge 6]\)?
  5. Determine the mean of the probability distribution by using \(\mu = \sum x\cdot \mathbb{P}[x]\).
  6. Determine the standard deviation of the probability distribution by using \(\sigma = \sqrt{\sum (x-\mu)^2 \cdot \mathbb{P}[x]}\).
